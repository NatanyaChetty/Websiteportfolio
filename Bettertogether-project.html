The Better Together Campaign

 

 

 

 

 

Overview of the project

For this project we were introduced to Brand Activation and were split into groups with various campuses involved. Our client was called Masicorp who wanted to get exposure to their brand.

Executions

Tote Bags

The tote bag is a visual message for the campaignthat the customers can take home and donate towards the Masicorp brand.

Bag tag

The bag tag is a way of saying thank you to the customer for purchasing the product. it also gives the customer more information on the company that the money is going towards and this creates brand exposure. .

Social Media Posts

The advertisements in the following executions are to attract the attention of the audience and clearly display masicorp brand identity and is giving anidea on how it would attract the young generation and the target audience.
