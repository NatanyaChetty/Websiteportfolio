Overview of the project

For this project i was involved in the anual 2021 Brand Challenge at Vega College. we were split up into groups with other Vega students in SA and our client was Sally Williams. Their challenge for us was to make their brand appeal to the younger target. market.

Executions

Filter

The filter is a good interactive way to get in touch with the younger generation. I have created this for social media for users to reach their highscore and create brand awareness.

Prototypes

The protoypes show how the users would look like using the filter on Instagram and Facebook .

How it tied together

The filter create brand specific imagery that create amazing user generated content that Sally Williams can continuously use for social pages in the future. The filter also increase brand and product awareness while driving traffic to Sally Williams social media pages in a fun, young and interactive way. All in all the filter are a way to create online shareable moments between friends and family that spark joy.
