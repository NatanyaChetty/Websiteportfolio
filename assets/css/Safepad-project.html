The Safepad Campaign

 

 

 

 

 

Overview of the project

The purpose of this brief is to create a campaign for SafePad with the objective to raise awareness of the impact of period poverty and promote this product as the solution that solves real life problems which women experience.

Executions

Infographic/Pamphlet

Infographic and the Pamphlet raises awareness about period poverty in Africa as well as what the brand does to solve the problem by producing a re-usable sanitary pad.Not only does it teach readers about SafePad, the pamphlet itself also folds in the same way SafePad encourages users to fold their product as use - teaching them an important habit in looking after their SafePad.

Mockup Website

The design solution that was put into the website and the animation video is to show and create awareness that many young females donâ€™t have sanitary products. The executions connect with the user and makes them under- stand it is a common struggle amongst lives. The animation is a real story and we wanted to highlight that Safepad does help and benefit young females and can change lives. .

Animation

The storyline for the animation was kept simple, but was written to relay a strong message about the product. We wanted to show how SafePad could drastically change the lives, fu- tures and education of young women all over the world, but specially for ones who are less fortunate and live in rural areas.
