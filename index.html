About

Hello! I'm Natanya a Digital Designer, a branding, illustrator and fashion enthusiast, and a slighty obsessed for high quality and elegant designs. I’m currently available for freelance work

.My passion and skills are branding, animating and motion graphics, design and fashion editorial and photography. I would hope to broaden my knowledge and experience on designing and use it in the different industries.

If you have a project that you want to get started or think you need my help with something, then get in touch.

What i do

Front-end

As a designer , I have experience in working with softwares such as Adobe Suite 6 specifically Illustration, Photoshop, Premier Pro and After Effects, InDesign and Blender.

Back-end

I had the opportunity to design for clients such as Makeup Artists, Business managers and Decor Stores and in my free time designing magazine articles.

Freelance Design

I am available to do designing for magazine editorial and illustrations as that is something i want as a career. I use my time to advance my skills and keep up with the trends to improve my skills.
