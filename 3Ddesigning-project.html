Overview of the project

This project required us to create a 3D model of a product and create the store and shelves. The product that was chosen was a polariod

Executions

Polariod

The polariod was a choice because of its structure, i decided to go woth a colourful patter and a more old school design with the big lense.

Shelving

The shelving was inspired by movie and chocolate stands to give it that old vintag but to still be presented on modern stores. .

Store

The design for the store was a basic layout using a square shape. The colorscheme was a simple black, white and grey to make the product stand out.
